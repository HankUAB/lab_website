Advancing Lung Biology

The Yuan Lab focuses on how specialized cells in the airway—specifically pulmonary ionocytes—maintain the delicate balance of fluid and pH in the lungs. Our mission is to translate basic cell biology into life-changing treatments for Cystic Fibrosis and chronic lung diseases.

What we do

Research Areas

Ionocyte Physiology

Deciphering the molecular "crosstalk" between ionocytes and basal cells in the airway epithelium.

pH Regulation

Investigating how the ASL pH affects mucus viscosity and bacterial defense mechanisms.

Novel Therapeutics

Testing gene-editing and small-molecule approaches to restore ionocyte function in CF models.
