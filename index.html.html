Feng Yuan Lab

Investigating pulmonary ionocytes and epithelial transport in airway physiology and Cystic Fibrosis.

Research Focus

Pulmonary Ionocytes

We study the role of pulmonary ionocytes, a rare cell type discovered in the airway epithelium, and how they coordinate CFTR-mediated fluid balance.

Airway Surface Liquid

Our research investigates the mechanisms of pH regulation and chloride transport that maintain the airway surface liquid (ASL) properties essential for mucociliary clearance.

Cystic Fibrosis

Using primary human bronchial epithelial cells and advanced imaging, we explore therapeutic targets to restore lung function in patients with CF.
