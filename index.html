Feng Yuan, PhD

Assistant Professor | Division of Pulmonary, Allergy, and Critical Care Medicine

Our laboratory at the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB) investigates the cellular and molecular mechanisms of airway epithelial physiology. We are specifically interested in pulmonary ionocytes and their role in regulating airway surface liquid (ASL) through CFTR-mediated ion transport.

Research Interests

Ionocyte Biology

Studying the specification and function of rare pulmonary ionocytes in the human airway and their contribution to Cystic Fibrosis pathology.

Epithelial Transport

Investigating how chloride and bicarbonate transport coordinate to maintain the pH and hydration of the lung's defense barriers.
